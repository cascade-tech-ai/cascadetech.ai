ABOUT US

Cascade Technologies is an AI research and consulting company specializing in real-time llm agents.

Agentic llm applications are difficult enough to build without the real-time constraint, but the additional pressures of real-time responses make them even more difficult. We specialize in those challenges, and can help you meet real-time requirements in your agentic applications with the combination of knowledge and experience in real-time methods, as well as custom built technologies to enable real-time llm applications.

TECHNOLOGY

To meet the demands of real-time agentic applications you can't simply rely on off the shelf technology. The serial, autoregressive nature of llms makes them much more conducive to throughput than low latency. Cascade Technologies is making contributions to major open source projects such as VLLM to enable low latency llm inference. Predicted Outputs are the first of many such projects on that front.

But general purpose platforms like VLLM are simply not designed for low latency inference. We are building a custom llm inference platform, designed from the ground up for low latency inference. The core technology behind that effort is Flow Prompting. Flow is a simple, powerful language for context management and inference control. Flow allows ai engineers to use tools like structured outputs, parallelism, and predicted outputs to break free of the constraints of autoregressive inference and reduce latencies to the bare minimmum.
