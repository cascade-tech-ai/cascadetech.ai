NEXT GEN AI INFERENCE

Cascade Technologies is building a revolutionary new LLM inference platform. The era of the dominant LLM inference modality introduced by OpenAI has proven more long lasting than expected. But in reality it is anachronistic, limiting, and ultimately only constricts the promise of these marvelous machines.

At their core, LLMs are a dynamic, elegant, and even poetic guide through the probability space of human thought. Contemporary LLM inference modalities are such a tragic constriction of this potential! LLM inference APIs up to this point have taken the form of a message in a bottle, drifting back and forth across a lonely sea between human and machine intelligence.

'Flow prompting' is an attempt to bridge that ocean, to make human-LLM correspondence less of a transaction and more of a dance, or at least more of a real conversation. A good conversation doesn't have the rigid structure of a protocol, it is a constant flow of information and ideas between two parties, each reacting to the ideas of the other. Flow Prompting helps make llm inference more of a conversation and less of a protocol.

You'll have to forgive the obtuse metaphors for the moment. Code will follow soon, but for the moment, maybe reflect on what you think those ideas might look like if you were asked to implement them!
